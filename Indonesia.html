7-Day Indonesia Itinerary

Explore the best of Indonesia in 7 days

Day 1: Arrival in Bali

  • Arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport
  • Check-in at hotel in Ubud
  • Relax and explore the Ubud Monkey Forest
  • Evening sunset at Tanah Lot Temple

Day 2: Bali - Cultural Exploration

  • Visit Ubud Palace and traditional market
  • Explore the Tegallalang Rice Terraces
  • Visit Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary
  • Traditional Balinese dance performance in Ubud

Day 3: Bali - Beach Day

  • Morning relaxation at Kuta Beach
  • Water sports (surfing, snorkeling, etc.)
  • Visit Seminyak for shopping and dinner

Day 4: Flight to Yogyakarta

  • Fly from Bali to Yogyakarta
  • Explore the Sultan’s Palace (Kraton)
  • Visit the famous Borobudur Temple
  • Explore Malioboro Street for local food and souvenirs

Day 5: Yogyakarta - Prambanan & Mt. Merapi

  • Visit the Prambanan Temple Complex
  • Optional jeep tour to Mt. Merapi (volcano adventure)
  • Evening cultural performance at the Ramayana Ballet

Day 6: Flight to Jakarta

  • Fly from Yogyakarta to Jakarta
  • Visit the National Monument (Monas)
  • Explore Jakarta’s old town (Kota Tua)
  • Enjoy a river cruise along the Ciliwung River

Day 7: Jakarta - Shopping and Departure

  • Visit the Grand Indonesia Mall for shopping
  • Explore the Jakarta Cathedral and Istiqlal Mosque
  • Evening departure from Soekarno-Hatta International Airport

Have a great trip to Indonesia!
